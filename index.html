Electrosmart

ElectroSmart is an IoT home automation and energy management system. It uses PHP to connect ESP32 devices with a web interface and SQL databases for secure data storage. With HTML, JS, and CSS ,it offers remote appliance control and real-time insights into electricity consumption and costs. Designed for user-friendliness and sustainability, ElectroSmart empowers users to optimize energy usage and make informed decisions. Future enhancements aim to expand compatibility, integrate AI analytics, and incorporate renewable energy solutions.
